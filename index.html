// Our Mission

The Bay Area
Needs This.

The Bay Area is home to over 500,000 middle and high school students, yet fewer than 10 active independent marching groups remain. Lack of funding, declining school program participation, and inaccessible instruction are limmiting one of the most unique art forms in competitive music.

On The Line was built to change that. By connecting students directly with working professionals, we're making elite instruction accessible to every student, at every level, regardless of what school or group they belong to.
